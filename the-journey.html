Establishing quantifiable domains

EXPERT SELECTION

Participants from various professional organizations were asked to nominate experts with optimistic, pessimistic, and realistic perspectives on the future of human welfare and societal change.

Gathered from different corners of the globe, 28 distinguished scholars nominated 68 experts on human welfare. 34 experts were selected from the 68 based on expertise and ensuring discipline diversity in the sample. An additional 66 experts we identified to reach a target sample pool of 100.

INITIAL DOMAIN SELECTION
