Futurescape

A collaborative research initiative aimed at predicting the future of human welfare. Through a longitudinal forecasting tournament, we focus on debates over societal change and draw on the predictions of experts, keen-eyed “superforecasters” and everyday people.

Predicting tomorrow's world 

Gathered from different corners of the globe, a target pool of 100 experts were able to identify 8 initial domains based on their opinions, team discussion, and relevance to human progress. They individually ranked the eight preselected domains by perceived importance, narrowing them to the final four domains with the most reliable markers.
